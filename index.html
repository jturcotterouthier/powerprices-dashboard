i Data Source: Day-ahead hourly prices from Energy-Charts API (Fraunhofer ISE), sourced from EPEX Spot / Nord Pool. Endpoint: api.energy-charts.info/price?bzn={market}. Data updates daily after the EPEX day-ahead auction (~12:45 CET). Resolution: 15-min or 60-min depending on bidding zone. License: CC BY 4.0.
σ Calculations: KPI cards — Avg Price (7d) = 7-day simple moving average of daily mean prices. Avg Spread (7d) = 7d SMA of daily (max-min). BESS Revenue = spread x 0.88 RTE, 1 cycle/day. Neg Hours = hours with price < €0. Volatility σ = intraday sample std deviation. Solar Cannibalisation Index = avg price 10-14h / avg price 24h. Peak/Off-Peak Ratio = avg 08-20h / avg 20-08h.
☀ Solar Asset Owner: Focus on Solar Cannibalisation Index, Negative Hours, and Avg Price trend.
🔋 BESS Asset Owner: Focus on BESS Revenue (7d), Cumulative BESS Revenue, Avg Spread, and Volatility σ.

Revenue duration curve — shows what percentage of days achieve a given spread level
i Calculations: Daily spread = max - min. Sorted descending as duration curve. P50 = median, P90 = spread exceeded on 10% of days.
Solar cannibalisation index = midday (10-14h) avg / baseload (24h) avg. Values below 1.0 indicate solar-driven price depression.
i Calculations: Peak avg = mean 08-20h. Off-peak avg = mean 20-08h. Solar avg = mean 10-14h. All plotted as 7-day rolling averages.
σ Calculations: Daily σ = sample std deviation of intraday prices. Red dots = outlier days (>2σ from mean). Negative hours = intervals with price < €0.
i Data: Raw intraday prices per day plotted hour 0-24. Highlight splits overlay into two colour groups.
i Heatmap: Each cell = one hour of one day. Colour gradient from global min (cool) to max (warm).
